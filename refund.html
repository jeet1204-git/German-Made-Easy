Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last updated: February 2024

Policy Overview

Refund policies will be determined and communicated upon course launch. As we are currently in development phase, specific refund terms will be established before the first course goes live.

General Guidelines

We are committed to student satisfaction. Our refund policy will consider:

  • The extent of course content accessed by the student
  • The time elapsed since purchase
  • Technical issues or access problems
  • Legitimate concerns about course quality or content accuracy

How to Request a Refund

To request a refund once our policy is established, please contact us at german.made.easy12@gmail.com with:

  • Your full name and registered email
  • Order/Payment ID
  • Reason for refund request

Processing Time

Approved refunds will typically be processed within 7-10 business days and credited to the original payment method.

Contact Us

For any questions about refunds or cancellations, please email us at german.made.easy12@gmail.com
